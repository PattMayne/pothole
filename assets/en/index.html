What is this?

This is a Fediverse instance running Pothole. Pothole is a fast microblogging server that has the ability to communicate with ActivityPub-compatible social networks such as Mastodon servers and Pleroma, Misskey, Friendica instances.

How do I get started?

Pothole does not come with a frontend installed by default, although, you can use any Mastodon client you want!
